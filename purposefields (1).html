Services

Career coaching is at the centre.
Wellbeing support surrounds it.

Two distinct but connected offerings. Career coaching is the primary practice. Wellbeing services sit alongside it — available as standalone support or woven into the coaching relationship.

All services are delivered online. The right combination is explored in your free discovery call. There's no fixed package — the work is always shaped around what you actually need.
Primary Service

Career Coaching

Whether you're navigating a crossroads, building your professional identity, or ready to make a move — career coaching is where direction, strategy, and authentic expression come together. The work draws on career construction theory, ACT, psychometric tools, and ten years of practice across government, nonprofits, and the private sector.

Also offered

Wellbeing Services

Holistic support for the whole person — not just the career. These services can stand alone or integrate naturally alongside coaching work, depending on what you need.

Wellbeing Coaching

Structured support for sustained energy, clarity, and balance. For people who want to function well — not just cope.

Psychotherapy

Therapeutic support grounded in ACT and relational approaches. Somatic bodywork and sound healing are available as adjuncts within this work.

Resilience Training

Building the psychological foundations — nervous system regulation, adaptive coping, and the inner resources that make sustained effort possible.

Intuitive Reading

Gene Keys, Tarot & Oracle Card Readings, and I Ching Divination — for people drawn to understanding themselves through a deeper, more archetypal and symbolic lens.

Meditation Training

Personalised instruction in meditation practice — from foundational technique to deepening an existing practice. Supports nervous system regulation, clarity, and sustained inner stability.

Resistance Healing

Targeted work to identify and dissolve the internal blocks that show up as procrastination, avoidance, or self-sabotage. Draws on somatic, psychological, and energetic approaches to mitigate the impact of adverse experiences and childhood patterns — so they stop running the show when it matters most.

About

Vikashan (Vik)

Career Counsellor · Psychotherapist · ICF-Accredited Coach · Sydney

B Psych Hons, UNSW Counselling & Psychotherapy ICF-Accredited Coach Career Development ACT Practitioner Gene Keys

I'm a career counsellor, psychotherapist, and integrative practitioner with over a decade of experience working across government, nonprofits, and the corporate world. I understand what it is to build a career that looks right on paper but feels hollow in practice — and I know what it takes to change course.

My practice sits at the intersection of psychology, soma, and spirit. I draw on ACT, career construction theory, Gene Keys, Tarot, I Ching, and somatic practice — not as a menu, but as a living toolkit used intuitively based on what each person actually needs. Sessions can be cognitive, therapeutic, intuitive, or somatic — often all of these across a working relationship, shaped by what emerges.

Sliding scale pricing is core to how I work, not an afterthought. Good support should not be a privilege. I built this practice as a social enterprise because I believe that.

Pricing

Sessions from $150 per hour.

The full rate is $150–$220 per hour. An accessible rate ($75–$150) is available for those who need it, and concession rates (under $75) by application. Choose based on your honest financial situation.

Full Rate
$150–$220
per hour
For those with financial stability. This is the full rate and the preferred tier — choosing it helps sustain the practice and subsidise access for others.
Accessible Rate
$75–$150
per hour
For those who need a lower rate to access support. Pay anywhere in this range. Choosing a higher amount within the band helps preserve capacity for others.
Concession
Under $75
by application
For those experiencing financial hardship or receiving income support. Limited spots each month — a short application helps manage the waitlist fairly.

All sessions online via Zoom. In-person options (Sydney & Northern Rivers) available by arrangement — contact Vik directly. Payment plans available on request.
